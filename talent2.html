I have been playing the piano ever since I was 8 years old. This is my maine instrument, and I have played it more than any other instrument.




I play the trumpet in my high school's jazz and symphonic band, and I am first chair in both. In jazz band, the first trumpet sets the style and volume for the entire band, so it's a big responsibility.




I started playing guitar during covid, and it's probably the most relaxing of the three to play. It's much easier to sit down and sing while playing the guitar than on either of the other two instruments I play. Additionally, it has lead me to discover I enjoy playing bass as well, so I might be able to add that to my list of instruments soon.
